Audio Guestbook FAQ

Read our Audio Guestbook FAQ below:

Q: What is an audio guestbook?

A: An audio guestbook is a unique way to capture messages from your guests at a special event, such as a wedding or anniversary party. Instead of writing in a traditional guestbook, your guests can record a personal message that is then compiled into a keepsake album.

Q: How does the audio guestbook work?

A: Our audio guestbook kit comes with a recording device and a beautiful album. Simply place the recording device on a table or stand and invite your guests to record a message. After the event, you'll receive all the recordings and can add them to your album.

Q: How long can the messages be?

A: The recording device allows for messages up to 3 minutes in length.

Q: How do I receive the recordings?

A: We will provide you with a digital file containing all the recordings, as well as a link to a private online gallery where you can listen to and download the recordings.

Q: How long does it take to receive my audio guestbook?

A: We aim to deliver your audio guestbook within 1 week of your event.

Q: Can I add more recordings to my audio guestbook after the event?

A: Yes, you can add more recordings to your album after the event. Simply send us the additional recordings and we'll add them to your digital file.

Q: What if I have a problem with my audio guestbook?

A: We strive to ensure that our customers are completely satisfied with their audio guestbooks. If you have any problems, please contact us and we will do our best to resolve the issue.
