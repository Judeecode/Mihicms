BACKGROUND

Pure Storage, a leading provider of enterprise-grade storage solutions, aimed to enhance its brand experience and engagement at a large-scale trade show.

They sought to create an immersive and interactive experience that integrated AI technology and allowed participants to feel more connected to their brand.

PARTNERSHIP

Pure Storage collaborated with MiHi Entertainment, a creative and experiential marketing agency known for its unique approach to creating cutting-edge, immersive experiences.

MiHi Entertainment's expertise in integrating AI technology and crafting engaging experiences made them the ideal partner for Pure Storage.

THE

DATA HEROES
CAMPAIGN

Together, MiHi Entertainment and Pure Storage developed the Data Heroes campaign. MiHi Entertainment designed backdrops, generated art for the Pure Storage website, created website graphics, and built a fully interactive experience for Pure Storage's booth.

The Data Heroes campaign featured an AI-powered photo booth with a villain character to emphasize the importance of transitioning from hard discs to cloud storage. This activation provided a fun and engaging way for participants to connect with the brand and learn more about Pure Storage's offerings.

WITH MIHI ENTERTAINMENT

AI ANALYTICS QUIZ

To further enhance the engagement, MiHi Entertainment developed an AI Analytics Quiz. Participants answered questions that helped differentiate their character, and the analytics provided insights into potential clients, vendors, and more.

The AI Analytics Quiz provided valuable data to Pure Storage while offering participants a personalized and interactive experience.

RESULTS

The collaboration between Pure Storage and MiHi Entertainment resulted in a successful and memorable event experience that generated buzz around Pure Storage's brand. Participants enjoyed the unique experience and the opportunity to engage with AI technology in a fun and accessible way.

The Data Heroes campaign was so successful that it evolved into a large-scale trade show product, showcasing the potential of AI integration in experiential marketing. Additionally, the campaign caught the attention of a keynote speaker who commended Pure Storage and MiHi Entertainment for their responsible use of AI.
