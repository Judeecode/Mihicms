What is a Claw Machine?

A claw machine is a classic arcade game that's all about skill, luck, and excitement. Guests use a joystick to control a mechanical claw, trying to grab a prize from inside the machine and drop it into the winning slot. It's a fun, interactive experience that keeps people coming back for more while creating a lively and playful atmosphere at any event.

Using our claw machines is simple and stress-free. We handle the setup, so all you need to do is stock it with your chosen prizes and let your guests take turns testing their luck and skill. Whether you're encouraging friendly competition, rewarding attendees, or adding a nostalgic touch to your event, our claw machine rentals are guaranteed to bring excitement and engagement to any occasion.

We'll bring the claw machine straight to your venue, right on time and ready for the fun.

Our crew will handle the setup process, so your guests can jump in and start playing right away.

Once the event is over, we'll safely pack everything up for removal.
