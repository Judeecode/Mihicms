Frequently asked questions

Have questions about our Money Booth Rentals? We're happy to answer them!

  • What goes inside the booth?

    • Branded bills, promo vouchers, golden ticketsanything lightweight that guests can grab. Well help you customize the contents.

    • One person at a time keeps the excitement focused and ensures safety. Each session lasts 1530 seconds.
