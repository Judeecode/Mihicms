FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q:

How does our SketchBot Work?

+
A:

Guests take a photo, and our SketchBot transforms it into a custom line-art sketch drawn live with a robotic arm. The entire process happens in real-time, allowing guests to watch as their portrait comes to life stroke by stroke on paper.

Q:

Can SketchBot work with photos from other booths?

+
A:

Absolutely! Our Photo Activations, AI Booth, and Roaming Booth can send images directly to SketchBot for instant artistic transformation. This seamless integration allows you to create a complete photo-to-sketch experience at your event.

Q:

How Long Does Each Sketch Take?

+
A:

On average, each sketch takes 30 seconds to 1 minute depending on detail and style. Simpler line-art sketches are faster, while more detailed or stylized sketches may take closer to a minute. The robotic arm works with precision speed to ensure guests don't wait long.

Q:

Do guests get to keep the sketches?

+
A:

Yes! Each guest receives a physical copy of their sketch as a unique keepsake. Digital versions can also be shared via email or SMS for easy sharing on social media. It's the perfect combination of a tangible art piece and digital convenience.

Q:

Can we customize the sketches with branding or logos?

+
A:

Yes, each sketch can include a branded overlay, logo, or custom template that matches your event theme. We can incorporate your company colors, event branding, or special messaging directly into every sketch, making it a personalized branded experience for your guests.

Q:

Is SketchBot suitable for large events?

+
A:

Yes, SketchBot is great for high-traffic events! We can set up a queue system or use pre-captured images to keep things moving efficiently. For very large events, we can optimize the sketching process or work with pre-captured photos to ensure every guest leaves with their custom artwork without long waits.
